Ecuador

Quilotoa is a stunning volcanic crater lake located in the Andes of Ecuador. The lake was formed after a massive volcanic eruption about 800 years ago, and it is now filled with turquoise-colored water. Quilotoa is a popular tourist destination because of its breathtaking views, hiking trails, and the opportunity to experience indigenous culture in nearby villages. The area is peaceful and perfect for nature lovers and adventurers.
